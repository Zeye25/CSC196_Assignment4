More Interesting Facts about Badminton:

  • Badminton is officially the fastest racquet sport in the world.
  • The sport of badminton originated in ancient Greece and was called "battledore and shuttlecock."
  • The shuttlecock, also known as a birdie, can reach speeds of up to 200 miles per hour.
  • Badminton made its debut as an Olympic sport in 1992.
  • The longest badminton match on record lasted for more than 2 hours and 30 minutes.
